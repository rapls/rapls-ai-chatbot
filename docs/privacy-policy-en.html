Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 20, 2025

Rapls Works ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our website and related products/services (including WordPress plugins and mobile/desktop applications).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Information submitted via our contact form (Contact Form 7), such as your name, email address, and message content
  • Information related to payments (Stripe), such as purchaser details and transaction information necessary to process your order
  • Analytics data collected via Google Analytics (using cookies), such as pages viewed, device/browser information, referrers, and partial IP information
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide that is necessary to deliver our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries, provide support, and communicate with you
  • To provide, operate, maintain, and improve our products and services
  • To process purchases, payments, refunds, and related customer support
  • To prevent fraud, ensure security, and troubleshoot technical issues
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • For purposes incidental to the above

3. Contact Form (Contact Form 7)

We use Contact Form 7 to handle inquiries. The information you submit through the form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not used for unrelated purposes.

For spam prevention, we may implement additional security services (e.g., reCAPTCHA). If we do, we will update this policy or provide a separate notice.

4. Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used and to improve it. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect traffic data. This data does not directly identify you.

You can refuse the collection of cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features.

For more information on how Google handles data, please refer to Google's policies.

5. Payments (Stripe)

We use Stripe to process payments for paid products and services. In connection with payment processing, Stripe may handle purchaser and transaction information. We use such information only to fulfill transactions (payment confirmation, customer support, refunds, etc.).

For details, please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies for analytics and site functionality. Cookies are small data files stored on your browser and do not typically contain information such as your name, address, or email address by themselves.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not provide your personal information to third parties without your consent, except where permitted or required by law.

8. Service Providers

We may outsource certain operations to trusted service providers to the extent necessary to achieve the purposes described above. In such cases, we will take reasonable measures to supervise and ensure appropriate handling of personal information. (Examples include Stripe for payment processing and Google for analytics.)

9. Security

We take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure of personal information.

10. Your Rights (Access / Correction / Deletion)

If you request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction on the use of your personal information, we will respond within a reasonable period in accordance with applicable laws, after verifying your identity. In some cases, we may not be able to comply due to legal requirements.

11. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. Please review the privacy policies of the linked sites.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed due to changes in laws or our operations. If there are material changes, we will post a notice on our website.

13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or the handling of personal information, please contact us via our inquiry form.
